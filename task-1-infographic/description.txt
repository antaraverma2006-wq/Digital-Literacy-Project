I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The infographic focuses on explaining digital literacy in a simple and visual way. It includes sections such as the meaning of digital literacy, its importance for students, safe internet practices, and basic email etiquette. I used icons and short bullet points to make the content easy to understand and visually appealing. The design uses a clean layout with soft colors to keep it readable. While creating it, I initially found it challenging to organize the layout properly, but after adjusting the placement of elements, the final design became more clear and effective.
